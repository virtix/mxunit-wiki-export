h2. MXUnit 2.0

* Adobe ColdFusion 8.x and later (ColdFusion 9 recommended)
* Railo
* Open Blue Dragon
* Windows, Linux, Mac
* Recommended: Eclipse, CFEclipse, or ColdFusion Builder

{note}
For Railo and Open Blue Dragon, _most_ features work as expected, however, some features
(such as the DataProviders) do not work as they would on Adobe ColdFusion.

Please file compatibility issues, and patches :-), at http://jira.mxunit.org/secure/IssueNavigator.jspa?reset=true&amp;mode=hide&amp;pid=10000
{note}

h2. MXUnit 1.x

* Adobe ColdFusion 7.x and later
* Railo
* Open Blue Dragon
* Windows, Linux, Mac
* Recommended: Eclipse, CFEclipse, or ColdFusion Builder
